About Me:

Not all paths lead us in a direct route to our ture calling. This year has been full of challenges for many but I beleive it has also given people the ability to push themselves beyond their comfort zones. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Nuclear Medicine Technology, a Minor in Human Resource Management and a Certificate of Leadership and Supervision, from Ferris State University, where I began to carve my path as a healthcare worker. After 5 years of working at hospitals I accepted a job offer at a Nuclear Cardiology software company which sparked a strong interest in healthcare applications. I was lucky enough to come across the MSU Coding Bootcamp for an opportunity to learn, challenge and advance my professional coding and development skills. While I believe a career makes up a good portion of your identity, what you do outside of work really tells a story. If I'm not working you'll find me on a boat or running around a lake. As a born and raised Michigander, I try my best to enjoy all four seasons! I love mountain biking, snowboaring, and some good wine tasting in Traverse City. I'm excited to see where my coding and deveoplement skills take me and can't wait to create something great!
